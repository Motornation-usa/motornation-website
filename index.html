FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to do anything to prepare? ▾

Nope! Just make sure your vehicle is parked and accessible at the address you provide. We bring everything — oil, filter, tools, and supplies.

How do I pay? ▾

We accept cash, all major credit/debit cards, Venmo, and Zelle. Payment is collected on the day of service after the work is complete.

What if my vehicle needs more than 5 quarts of oil? ▾

The Basic package covers up to 5 quarts. If your vehicle takes more, we recommend the Premium or Elite packages which cover up to 7 quarts.

What areas do you service? ▾

We serve Aurora, Denver, Centennial, Englewood, Lakewood, Littleton, Thornton, Westminster, Arvada, and Commerce City.

How soon can I get an appointment? ▾

We typically have availability within 1–3 days. Same-day appointments may be available depending on the schedule.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule? ▾

No problem — just text or call us at least a few hours before your appointment and we'll get you rescheduled at no charge.
